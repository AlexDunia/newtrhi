The Rationale

The idea behind this initiative was borne out of the purchase of numerous expensive fabrics for parties and ceremonies, the clothes made out of these fabrics are rarely used with some worn once or twice in years and may never be worn again. Whereas there are thousands of people without such to wear for the ceremony of their newborn. Instead of gving it out to them outrightly, we can sell at a giveway rate and use the money generated to take a child off the street.

Our Strategy

D - DONATE items not in use to TRHI

S - SELL at a giveaway price to generate revenue

T - TAKE an Adolescent off the streets by training

E - EMPOWER for self sustainability

N - NUTURE the beneficiary

Our vision

To empower adolescents who are willing to learn a vocation and improve their standard of living and that of their family. To train, establish and nuture hidden talents to become professionals for the good of themselves and the socity at large

Our vision is to raise diligent adolescents who in turn liberates their families and society.

We implore you to join the TRHI cause and donate items still in good condition but no longer in use

Thorough your donations, we hope to take over 150 teens off the streets in 5 years.
